RHOBH Season 11 Will Begin Filming Soon Heres Whos Returning And One Full-Time Cast Member

After another season of editors dragging one storyline throughout too many episodes to count, the Real Housewives of Beverly Hills have wrapped their season. Now, the race is on to secure a cast for the next installment of the franchise.

Denise Richards left after being the target in an alleged affair that Brandi Glanville claims they had. Although most would think Denise quit after being bullied, Andy Cohen claims that they just couldn’t negotiate a deal.

After three seasons, Teddi Mellencamp allegedly got the boot after not providing enough drama for the series. She admitted on her Instagram that the decision was not a mutual one; however, she was thankful for the opportunity.

Kyle Richards, Lisa Rina, Erika Jayne, Dorit Kemsley, Garcelle Beauvais are allegedly coming back in full-time capacity. Meanwhile, Sutton Stracke is set to return but it’s unknown if she will be a friend of or a diamond holder.

After the canceling of Keeping Up With the Kardashians, many people wanted Kris Jenner to be on the show. However, she said that she wouldn’t be able to do it as a housewife or even a friend of but she’s definitely open to being a guest considering she’s friends with Kyle.

Now, rumors are swirling that Kathy Hilton will be joining the Beverly Hills women as a friend.

Hollywood Life also reported that they are currently on the hunt for one more full-time member.

‘As of now, there has been talk about bringing in one new wife next season. She has not been cast yet, but the network is talking to people right now. The ladies have been told to clear their schedules to begin filming at the end of October. That could change, but that’s the plan. They’ll all be coming back except for Teddi and obviously Denise who left at her own choosing.’
