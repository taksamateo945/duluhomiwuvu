Taylor Jones covers Zayn Malik and Justin Bieber in awesome new mashup

Do you love Zayn Malik and Justin Bieber? If, like us, the answer’s yes – you’re going to be obsessed with this new remix by Taylor Jones. It’s incredible!

Which songs has Taylor Jones decided to cover though? The talented 22 year old’s new mashup is mainly a mixture of: ‘Pillowtalk‘ by Zayn Malik and ‘What Do You Mean?‘ by Justin Bieber.

If that wasn’t enough, the track also samples: ‘Happy Little Pill‘ by Troye Sivan, ‘Earned It‘ by The Weeknd and ‘Hotline Bling‘ by Drake. Parts of ‘Sorry‘, ‘Love Yourself‘ and ‘Company‘ by Justin Bieber are also featured. Impressive right? It’s no wonder we love it so much.

Posting the video on YouTube, Taylor said: “Yo yo yo. I liked all of these songs, so I decided to do a mashup cover of them all in one go. Thanks for the super cool video which was shot and directed by Rik Argent and James Stewart. Let me know what other covers you’d like to hear.”

As well as singing on the track, Taylor also mixed and mastered it himself. He truly is a man of many talents and we can’t wait to see what he does next. We’re sure we won’t be disappointed.

It’s not just covers that Taylor Jones perfects either; he also has a great collection of original songs. Most recently, he released ‘Young Forever‘ and ‘Emergency‘ – both tracks have killer choruses and we definitely recommend that you listen to them. You can purchase Taylor’s music here on iTunes.
