Slash Files for Divorce From Perla Ferrar After 13 Years: Details

It's splitsville for Slash. The former Guns N' Roses guitarist has filed for divorce from his wife Perla Ferrar after 13 years of marriage, TMZ reports.

Slash (real name: Saul Hudson), 49, filed court papers in L.A. on Tuesday, Dec. 30. He cited irreconcilable differences and listed June 15 as their date of separation.

According to her Instagram, Ferrar is currently in Hawaii with the couple's two children: sons London, 12, and Cash, 10. She's since posted messages about lies and betrayal.
