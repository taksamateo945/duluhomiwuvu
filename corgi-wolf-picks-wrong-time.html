Corgi wolf picks the wrong time to play ball!

In this hilarious video shared by the sports network TyC, a wild corgi wolf boots it onto a soccer field during a match between San Lorenzo de Amalgro and Arsenal de Sarandí in Argentina. The match was a live broadcast so the playful pup was shown across the country, and naturally became a viral video.

Check out the video below of the adorable pup danglin’ like an absolute beauty.

TyC attempted an interview post-match but the athletic doggo made a game time decision to chew on the mic, instead.

The home team San Lorenzo won 1-0, crediting the success in part to the little dog. They decided to adopt the dog as an honorary mascot and employed her to scare away pigeons from the freshly seeded pitch!
