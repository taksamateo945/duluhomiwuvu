Alphonso Davies Biography, Story

The Soccer Player is best known as Alphonso Davies. Winger who started out with the MLS’s Vancouver Whitecaps FC in 2016. He was born in Buduburam, Ghana, on 2 November 2000. As he made his MLS debut in July 2016 he became the second youngest player behind Freddy Adu to play in the league. His youth career starts with Edmonton Internationals, Edmonton Strikers, and Vancouver Whitecaps FC. Born in the 2000s, he became the first player to appear in an MLS game.
