Career

  • In the early to mid-2000s, he was the guitarist for the Silver Spring, Maryland-based metal band PSI before joining the technical metalcore band Reflux. Prosthetic Records recognized his talent at a Reflux performance and gave him a record contract as a solo artist.
  • He recorded the debut Animals as Leaders CD in 2009 alongside Misha Mansoor (of Periphery), who produced and recorded the album with Tosin as well as programming the drums. After the record was finished, Javier Reyes and Navene Koperweis were brought in to play rhythm guitar and drums, respectively, transforming the project into a full-fledged touring band.
  • In 2011, Tosin started putting together material for a new band, T.R.A.M., consisting of his Animals As Leaders bandmate Javier Reyes, Suicidal Tendencies drummer Eric Moore, and former The Mars Volta wind instrumentalist Adrián Terrazas-González. “T.R.A.M.” stands for Terrazas, Reyes, Abasi, Moore.
  • Abasi performed on the Generation Axe tour with Nuno Bettencourt, Yngwie Malmsteen, Steve Vai, and Zakk Wylde in April and May 2016. The ensemble subsequently returned to Asia in 2017 then to North America in 2018.
