Reba McEntire, Husband Narvel Blackstock Split After 26 Years

Country superstar Reba McEntire and her husband, Narvel Blackstock, are separating after 26 years of marriage, a rep for the couple confirms to Us Weekly.

The singer, 60, and the manager and producer, 58, announced their split on Monday, Aug. 3, in a joint statement.

"Despite this being the end of their marriage, they continue to support each other," the statement from Big Machine Label Group read. "They have worked together for 35 years and will continue to do so. They have asked that you respect their privacy during this time."

According to the statement, the stars have already been separated for several months. A rep for Big Machine did not say whether the couple had filed for divorce.
