Charli DAmelio Is Rumored To Be Dating Travis Barkers Son Landon Barker

Landon Barker, Travis Barker’s 18-year-old son, may be dating Charli D’Amelio. According to a source close to the celebrity, the couple is in the early stages of dating. They stated:

“They’re dating, and it’s in its early phases.”

Since they were seen together on several occasions, fans have speculated about a relationship between D’Amelio and Barker. In the middle of June 2022, the two were observed exiting Barker’s performance together. However, the adolescent idols’ agents have yet to respond to the report.

Other incidents that fueled relationship rumors regarding Charli D’Amelio and Landon Barker

Aside from leaving Barker’s performance in June of this year, the two were also seen leaving a party in Los Angeles to commemorate D’Amelio’s sister Dixie’s first album, A Letter To Me. According to press reports, D’Amelio and Barker both uploaded Instagram posts about having tattoos from the same Los Angeles-based artist, Arbel (@ar.bel).

Who exactly is Landon Barker? Early life, career, and other topics

Landon Asher Barker is a fashion-forward American rapper, singer, musician, and social media celebrity. Barker rose to prominence in the MTV reality show Meet the Barkers, which ran from 2005 to 2006. The musician is well-known as a drummer and rapper, and he has over 1.2 million followers on Instagram. On October 9, 2003, he was born to Blink 182 drummer Travis Barker and his former girlfriend Shanna Moakler. The artist grew up in Santa Monica, California, in the United States.

Before enrolling at Fontana High School in California, he attended a private school in Santa Monica. The influencer is allegedly interested in acting and has participated in a number of school and community theatrical activities.
Alabama Luella Barker is the artist’s younger sister, while Atiana Cecilia De La Hoya is her elder half-sister. Barker portrayed a drummer in the short film Downfalls High, which was released in 2021. Mod Sun and Machine Gun Kelly directed the film.

He often uses the word OTG in front of his name, which stands for On The Grind. Trust, Don’t Need Her, Skulls Misfits, and Holiday are among the many songs recorded by the 18-year-old performer. He’s also collaborated with Purple, Rick Owens, Fashion Nova Men, and Famous Stars and Straps. Barker’s net worth is estimated to be between $3 and $4 million, according to newsunzip.com.

Who Has Charli D’Amelio previously dated?

Charli D’Amelio is a notable Gen Z influencer who rose to prominence via her TikTok videos. She was formerly linked to TikTok sensation Chase Hudson (a.k.a. musician Lil Huddy). They began dating in December 2019 and split up in April 2020. The news was shared with her admirers by the younger D’Amelio sister, who wrote:

“Since you’ve been following my and Chase’s relationship from the beginning, I felt compelled to inform you that we are no longer together. It pains me to say this, but we’ve decided that this is what’s best for both of us.”

She went on to say that the two were still friends:

“We are still good friends, and I wouldn’t trade that for the world! I really care for Chase and wish him nothing but the best. It makes me pleased to see all of the good things happening for him.”

Rumor has it that Hudson just erased a photo of himself with Barker from his Instagram site. Many saw this as another indicator of Charli D’Amelio and Landon Barker’s relationship.
