Lorenzo Zurzolo- Bio, Family, Relationship &amp; Career

Who is Lorenzo Zurzolo?

Lorenzo Zurzolo is an Italian actor. He is famously known for You Can’t Kiss the Bride, Under the Riccione Sun, Weekend, etc. In addition, he played Niccolò in the series Baby alongside actress Benedetta Porcaroli and Alice Pagani.

The handsome actor is very famous for his looks and has even gotten his modeling hands. With his blue eyes and wavy hair, he has the teenage girls all swoon for him.

After working in small commercials and TV shows, Lorenzo Zurzolo has come along a very long way. He has got the looks and the talent, and undoubtedly with those deadly combinations, there will be many more groundbreaking projects to come from him shortly.

Lorenzo Zurzolo Early Life and Childhood

Lorenzo Zurzolo was born on March 21, 2000, in Rome, Italy. He is the second child of parents Federico Zurzolo and Gabria Cipulli. Moreover, he has an older sister named Ludovica Zurzolo, who is supposedly six years older than him.

Growing up in a place like Rome, Lorenzo has always been open to Italian culture and heritage. So it wouldn’t be a surprise to find how much value his heritage holds for him.

Ever since he was a child, he has always been fascinated with movies. He loved Italian cinema and soon fell in love with Hollywood as well. Moreover, he is also a fan of Japanese anime as he used to watch Dragon Ball Z very fondly as a child.

Parents

Lorenzo’s father, Federico Zurzolo, used to be the director of Radio 1. He later began working as a journalist. He lives in Italy with his family.

Gabria Cipulli (Mother)

Lorenzo’s mother, Gabria Cipulli, is a very successful woman. She owns a production company all by herself. So his mother might have had a hand in inspiring young Lorenzo to join the film industry.

Career

Lorenzo began his career in the show business at just seven years old. Then, he started to appear in commercials. This led him to choose this as his profession for the rest of his life.

He later played a small part in a TV show called Decameron di Daniele Luttazzi. Moreover, he began working in theaters at the age of 14. He contributed more to his acting career with continuous work in commercials, plays, and performances.

In 2012, Lorenzo got featured in a movie called Young Europe, which Matteo Vicino directed. He played the lead role in this movie, making it his first feature film. Moreover, he could act alongside Sophie Blondin and Michele Gammino.

With this movie, he began his career in Italian films. As a result, he became more familiar with the Italian crowd.

Another big success came to Lorenzo when he did a series called Baby. The show started in 2018 and ended after three seasons. However, it became known worldwide, and Lorenzo’s fans and fame rose to a great height.

He did other movies like Sotto il Sole di Riccione (Under the Riccione Sun). The actor played a blind character which was so convincing that the fans actually believed the actor to be blind in real life as well.

He also appeared in a thriller movie called Weekend in 2020. However, the film did not live up to its expectation. But, Lorenzo was still able to shine due to his excellent acting.

Awards

Lorenzo won the ‘Leopoldo Trieste Prize’ for his movie 2017 movie ‘Sconnessi.’ In 2020, he won Magna Grecia Awards for best acting. Moreover, he also won the Astro Nascente Italian Cinema in Golden Graal Award of Italy.

Net Worth and Income
